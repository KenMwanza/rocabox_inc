About Rocabox Inc

Professional logistics and delivery services with a commitment to sustainability and efficiency.

FedEx Ground Contractor & ISP

Rocabox Inc is a certified FedEx Ground contractor operating from the FedEx Ground terminal at 7020 Van Buren Road, Syracuse, NY, under the leadership of Robert Williams Jr, CEO & Owner. The company specializes in both the Alternative Vehicle Program (AVP) and Pickup & Delivery (P&D) services, providing reliable logistics solutions to businesses and communities throughout Central and Northern New York.

As a FedEx ISP (Independent Service Partner), Rocabox Inc maintains high standards of service and efficiency in the FedEx Ground network, operating from its primary terminal in Syracuse while maintaining administrative headquarters at 445 Coffeen St in Watertown.

Sustainable Operations

As part of FedEx's AVP (Alternative Vehicle Program), Rocabox Inc utilizes personal vehicles for efficient last-mile delivery services.

Strategic Location

Based in Watertown, NY, Rocabox Inc serves communities across Northern New York and the surrounding regions.

About Rocabox Inc

Professional logistics and delivery services with a commitment to sustainability and efficiency.

FedEx Ground Contractor Since 2024

Rocabox Inc is a certified FedEx Ground contractor operating in Watertown, New York. We specialize in both the Alternative Vehicle Program (AVP) and Pickup & Delivery (P&D) services, providing reliable logistics solutions to businesses and communities throughout Northern New York.

As a FedEx ISP (Independent Service Partner), we maintain high standards of service and efficiency in the FedEx Ground network.

Sustainable Operations

As part of FedEx's AVP program, we utilize alternative fuel and electric vehicles to reduce our environmental impact.

Strategic Location
